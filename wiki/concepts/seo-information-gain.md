# SEO Information Gain

SEO information gain is the useful, verifiable difference a page adds beyond generic or competitor content. The source argues that in the AI era, information and tools are abundant, so scarce value comes from specific, accurate, evidence-backed information.

## Sources of Information Gain

For AI tool sites, useful information gain can come from:

- Real examples and outputs.
- Screenshots or product-flow evidence.
- Test data and benchmark comparisons.
- Model-specific or workflow-specific coverage.
- Use-case depth by industry, role, language, or scenario.
- Clear tradeoffs and decision criteria.
- Templates, prompts, demos, or downloadable assets.

## What It Avoids

- Generic AI-written explanations.
- Keyword-stuffed long text.
- Thin pages that only swap terms.
- Claims without evidence.
- Page templates with no task-specific value.

## Relation to pSEO

Information gain is the constraint that makes programmatic SEO viable. Scaling pages is useful only if each page has structured, differentiated, and maintainable value.
