# Compounding knowledge artifact

A compounding knowledge artifact is a maintained body of knowledge whose value increases as new sources, questions, contradictions, and syntheses are integrated into it.

In the LLM Wiki pattern, the wiki compounds because the agent does not discard the work done during each interaction. It records summaries, cross-links, comparisons, open questions, and contradictions as durable pages.

## Why It Matters

Without persistence, an LLM repeatedly reconstructs context from raw materials. With a compounding wiki, prior synthesis becomes part of the working substrate for future reasoning.
