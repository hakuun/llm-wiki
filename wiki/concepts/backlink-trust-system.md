# Backlink Trust System

The backlink trust system is the source's framing for off-site SEO. Backlinks act as external votes of trust, discovery paths for crawlers, and potential referral traffic sources.

## Backlink Value

The source identifies three value dimensions:

1. **Precise referral traffic** — visitors from the linking page are potential users or buyers.
2. **Authority** — the linking site is trusted or influential in the market.
3. **Topical relevance** — the linking site and page relate to the target site's subject.

It ranks precise traffic as the most important practical value, with authority and relevance also important.

## AI Tool Site 0-to-1 Channels

For new AI tool sites, the source highlights:

- AI directories and navigation sites.
- Review sites and list pages.
- Product launch communities.
- Guest posts.
- Newsletters and resource pages.
- Topic-relevant citations.

## Risks

Backlink growth should appear natural and diversified. Sudden spikes, concentrated sources, low-quality directories, spam forums, PBNs, or irrelevant links may create risk rather than durable trust.

## Measurement

Track:

- Referring domains.
- Dofollow links.
- Link source quality and relevance.
- First-seen timing relative to organic growth.
- Referral sessions and conversions.
