# Programmatic SEO for AI Tool Sites

Programmatic SEO for AI tool sites is the use of templates, structured data, and automation to publish many search-useful pages. The source emphasizes that pSEO should scale information gain, not thin keyword permutations.

## Formula

The source frames pSEO as:

> template × structured content data × automation

For AI tool sites, scalable dimensions may include:

- Function.
- Use case.
- Industry.
- Language.
- Model.
- Version.
- Region.
- Persona.

## Failure Mode

pSEO fails when pages are:

- AI-generated at high volume with low differentiation.
- Mostly identical except for swapped keywords.
- Missing extractable conclusions, tables, steps, examples, or evidence.
- Not useful enough for users to click or stay.
- Published faster than quality and indexation can be monitored.

## Safer Practice

A safer workflow:

1. Create a small set of high-quality pages.
2. Validate indexation, CTR, engagement, and conversion.
3. Extract fields and reusable content modules.
4. Build templates that include comparisons, steps, FAQ, schema, and update logic.
5. Publish in batches.
6. Monitor effective index rate and quality signals.
