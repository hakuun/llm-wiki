# Obsidian

Obsidian is a Markdown-based knowledge management application. In the LLM Wiki pattern, it serves as the browsing and visualization environment for the human while the agent maintains the underlying Markdown files.

## Role in this Wiki

- view and navigate wiki pages
- use wikilinks and graph view to inspect structure
- potentially use plugins such as Dataview or Marp later
